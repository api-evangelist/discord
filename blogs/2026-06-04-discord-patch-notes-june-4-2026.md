This patch notes update details performance, reliability, and usability improvements across Discord platforms. Key changes include a confirmation step for accidental DM calls on mobile, approximately 650 milliseconds faster desktop startup time, and a redesigned Account settings page. Voice invite embeds were also updated to show server details and channel participant counts with animated avatars.
